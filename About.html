About Us

Welcome to the Drunken Bunker – The largest social media gathering of drinking enthusiasts in the world! You have seen videos of our crazy cocktails and boozy treats all over Facebook, Youtube, Instagram and Twitter and now we have brought them all together under one roof, making them really easy for you to find!

Whether you are looking for an epic jungle juice for your next party or a tasty new mimosa to brighten up brunch – you have come to the right place! From creative jello shots to spiked desserts – we have it all right here. Not sure what to make with ingredients you have at home? Use our liquor cabinet! Simply enter all the ingredients you have on hand and we will do the work for you, giving you a list of mouth-watering creations you can make right away! From shots and shooters to sangrias and punches – the possibilities are endless.

Take a look at our themed drinks and impress your friends and family during the holidays! Valentine’s Day, St Patrick’s Day, Cinco De Mayo, Fourth of July, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Christmas and New Year’s – we got you covered! You can also search by color and strength making it super simple for you to find exactly what you are looking for.

Check out our Mixology playlist were we showcase some of the world’s best mixologists and their awesome creations. These guys truly are the gourmet chef’s of the cocktail world! Vodka lover? Try one of our tasty martinis. Tequila fan? Check out our margaritas! Are rum drinks your favorite? We’ve got more daiquiris and mojitos then you can imagine. Whatever your preference we have something just for you. Now take a look at our delicious, bright and creative drinks and get mixing! Most of our recipes come with an instructional video so it’s never been easier to try something new. Stay Tipsy friends and remember to always Drink Responsibly!
